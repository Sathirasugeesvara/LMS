Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23rd of November - 2025 — for LMS - Faculty of Applied Science

Welcome to LMS - Faculty of Applied Science. We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Personal information

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account or interact with the LMS. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Username and password (securely stored)
  • Profile details (e.g., photo, title, institution)
  • Contact information you choose to provide (phone, address)

1.2 Course and usage data

To deliver learning services we collect data about your activity, such as:

  • Courses enrolled and completion status
  • Progress, scores, quiz submissions, and certificates
  • Interaction logs (timestamps, actions taken)

1.3 Device and technical data

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use the platform, including IP address, browser type/version, operating system, device identifiers, and pages visited.

1.4 Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and local storage to remember preferences, enable sessions, and analyze usage. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work correctly.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and maintain your account and access to courses.
  • To track learning progress, grade work, and issue certificates.
  • To send important account notifications, updates, and support messages.
  • To personalize content and recommend courses.
  • To analyze usage and improve the platform.
  • To protect against fraud and maintain security.

3. Legal bases for processing (if applicable)

If you are in a jurisdiction that requires it (for example, GDPR regions), we process personal data based on your consent, our contractual necessity to provide services, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests (improving the platform, security).

4. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Authorized staff and instructors for course administration.
  • Service providers who help operate the LMS (hosting, analytics, email delivery).
  • Partners or institutions you choose to share data with (for example, your employer or school).
  • Law enforcement or regulators when required by law.

5. Data retention

We retain personal data as long as your account exists and as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can request deletion of your account and personal data (see section "Your rights").

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure servers, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure — if a breach occurs we will follow legal reporting requirements and notify affected users as required.

7. Your rights and choices

You may:

  • Access and update your account information in your profile settings.
  • Request export of your personal data in a common, machine-readable format.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Opt out of non-essential marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at sathirasugeesvara@gmail.com or use the account settings page.

8. Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. If you are an institution enrolling minors, you agree you have the necessary parental consents. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account without consent, contact us to remove their information.

9. Third-party links and embedded content

Our platform may contain links or embedded content from third parties (video hosts, payment processors). We are not responsible for their privacy practices — please review the privacy policies of those services before sharing personal information.

10. International data transfers

Your data may be stored or processed in countries other than your own. When transferring data internationally we use appropriate safeguards (standard contractual clauses, approved frameworks) where required.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We’ll post the updated policy on this page with the revised date. Continued use of the platform after changes means you accept the updated policy.
